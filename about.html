About Me

Hello, and welcome to my photography website! I am an amateur photographer represented on several stock sites, interested in nature, people and stock photography. With a full-time job, I sometimes find myself constrained to devote adequate time to photography. However, I try and make as much time as possible for this passion of mine.

Most of my stock photos have been made in and around my house in natural light, on family vacation travels, and in my home studio (with strobes). I used to primarily shoot with Canon DSLRs, but in 2019, I decided to go the mirrorless route with the Canon EOS R.

To view or purchase my stock photos take a look at my gallery on Shutterstock, one of the largest stock sites I contribute to. You can also view my galleries on Dreamstime,  Adobe Stock, and Istock (Getty Images) .
